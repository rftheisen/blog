# Great Godly Resources to Feed Your Soul ✝️

I believe it's important to learn technical skills and advance your career, but I believe it is even more important to also grow spiritually and in relationship with God. In this article I share some awesome resources that have helped me learn more about the Bible, God and advance in my walk with Christ. Even if you do not believe in a God, these resources will serve as great motivation and perhaps provide alternative & progressive ways to look at life, yourself, purpose, learnership and relationships. This article will constantly evolve as I come across resources that are a blessing.
